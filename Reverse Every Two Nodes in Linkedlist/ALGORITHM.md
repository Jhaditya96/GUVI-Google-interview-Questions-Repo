Reverse Every Two Consecutive Elements of LinkedList
====================================================

<h3>
Statement :
</h3>
Reverse every 2 nodes in a singly linked list.

<h4>
Algorithm :
</h4>
Reverse every two nodes and that's all what is the algorithm.


<h4>
Complexity :
</h4>
Time : O(n) <br>
Space : O(1)
